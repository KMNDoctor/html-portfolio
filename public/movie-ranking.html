My Top 3 Movies

Avengers: Endgame

One of the most hyped build up ever for a movie, even the trailers were insane.
Was a lifetime long build up, don't know where it can go from now

Everything Everywhere All At Once

A sort of emotional movie, that kinda of surprised me a lot.
I related to it a lot too, and it was super cool how it was a more of a indie film
It was a breath of fresh air compared to the other movies that came out at the time

The Dark Knight

Honestly I need to rewatch it again, but I know this movie was amazing.
The score, and visuals and just the camera work was phenomenal
